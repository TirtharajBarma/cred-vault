# CredVault OTP Flow Specification ## OTP Verification Page - **Route**: /verify-otp - **Context**: Shown immediately after a user submits the registration form. - **Goal**: Securely verify the user's email/phone with a one-time password. - **UI Elements**: - Heading: "Verify Your Identity" - Subtext: "We've sent a 6-digit code to your email." - Input: 6 separate digit boxes for the OTP. - Actions: "Verify & Secure Access" button. - Footer: "Didn't receive the code? Resend OTP" (with a countdown timer). - **Style**: Soft Morning Mist 2.0 (Glassmorphic card, split-screen layout). ## Updated Flow 1. **Register Page** -> Click "Create Account" -> Redirect to **OTP Verification Page**. 2. **OTP Verification Page** -> Successful verification -> Redirect to **Login Page**. 3. **Login Page** -> Successful login -> Redirect to **Dashboard**.
